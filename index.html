info About Lil peep:

Lil Peep is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and model. He has been called one of the first musicians of the post-emo revival, and his hip-hop style became known as emo rap.

Born in Allentown, Pennsylvania and raised in Long Island, New York. Lil Peep started releasing music on SoundCloud in 2014 using the alias Lil Peep, as his mother called him "Peep" when he was a child. He soon became popular on SoundCloud after releasing several mixtapes and collaborating with Lil Tracy, which led to him gaining a cult following.

info About Flo Rida:

Tremar Dillard September 16, 1979 Carol City. Florida or Opa-loka, Florida, better known as Flo Raida, is an American rapper best known for the single "Low", which peaked at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 for 10 weeks in early 2008 and became the year's best-selling single in USA.

Flo Raida's first debut solo album was called Mail on Sunday. In early 2009, Flo Raida returned to number one on the charts with a cover version of the disco hit Right Round.

Sales of Flow Rida's music records have crossed the threshold of 75 million copies worldwide.
